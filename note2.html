Defensive Driving Techniques

When it comes to road safety, mastering defensive driving techniques is crucial. Here are some key strategies to enhance your safety on the road:

1. Maintain a safe following distance from the vehicle in front of you to allow for ample reaction time in case of sudden stops.

2. Stay alert and focused on the road at all times, scanning for potential hazards and anticipating other drivers' actions.

3. Avoid distractions such as using your phone, eating, or adjusting the radio while driving.

4. Use your turn signals and check your mirrors frequently to maintain awareness of your surroundings and communicate your intentions to other drivers.

5. Adhere to speed limits and traffic rules, and always be prepared for unexpected situations on the road.

By practicing these defensive driving techniques, you can protect yourself and others while enjoying a safe and pleasant driving experience!
